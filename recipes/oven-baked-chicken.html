Oven-baked chicken breast

This recipe is quick to make and ideal to prepare a few meals in advance. This is a no-fat version. You can add some olive oil to the mix if you want. Make sure to cover the dish with foil so the meat cooks slowly in the sauce and doesn't burn.

Preparation: 15 minutes

Cooking: 1 hour and a half at 350 deg. F (180 deg. C)

Ingredients

  • 4 or 5 chicken breasts
  • 1 small tin of tomatoe paste
  • A few sun-dried tomatoes (optional)
  • 1/3 cup soja sauce
  • Salt and pepper
  • 1 tbsp paprika
  • 1/2 tbs chili powder

Instructions

  1. Pre-heat the oven to 350 deg. F.
  2. Place the chicken breasts in a rectangular dish.
  3. Take a mixing bowl to prepare the sauce.
  4. Empty the tin of tomatoe paste in the bowl.
  5. Add the soja sauce.
  6. Cut the sun-dried tomatoes and add them to the bowl.
  7. Add the salt, pepper and spices.
  8. Stir well. This will make s thick sauce.
  9. Use a spatula to spread the sauce on each chicken breast.
  10. Cover the dish with foil.
  11. Place in the hot oven and leave to cook for up tp 2 hours.
