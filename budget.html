Weekly planner

How many meals are you going to prepare this week?
Write your shopping list according to the meals you plan to cook. Divide your list into sections:

  • Pantry
  • Meat
  • Dairy
  • Fruit & vegetables
  • Others (fruit juice, tea, coffee, bread, cereals, etc.)

While you are shopping

Keep your list handy and try to stick to it. Look for the deals in the meat, the vegetables, and the fruit sections.
Also:

  • By seasonal products.
  • Look for the deals, including items about to reach the eat-by-date.
  • Buy lumps of cheese instead of grated cheese.
  • Go for the raw ingredients instead of ready-made meals.

What to have in the fridge

  • Butter
  • Cheese
  • Milk
  • Big pot of greek or plain yogurt
  • Creme fraiche
  • Ham
  • Eggs
  • Meat: chicken breast, beef for stew, meat for a roast, etc...
  • Vegetables and fruit

What to keep in the pantry

  • Tins of peeled tomatoes
  • Tins of tuna
  • Olive oil
  • Salt, pepper and spices like papikra, red chili powder
  • Flour
  • Sugar
  • Honey
  • Pasta
  • Rice
  • Tea and coffee
  • Cereals
  • Bread
